VideoVault Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 16, 2026
Last updated: July 16, 2026

VideoVault is designed to process your media locally. VideoVault does not operate a developer server that receives or stores your videos, audio files, recordings, playlists, or backup contents.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how VideoVault: Media Player (“VideoVault,” “the app,” “we,” or “us”) handles information when you use the app, contact support, or purchase Lifetime Pro.

2. Media and app data stored on your device

VideoVault stores imported media, generated media, recordings, playlists, thumbnails, preferences, and related metadata inside the app’s local storage on your device.

  • VideoVault accesses photos or files only when you choose to import, save, export, share, back up, or restore content.
  • Microphone access is used only when you choose to record audio or create a voiceover.
  • Your media is not automatically uploaded to us.
  • VideoVault does not sell your media or app data.

3. Backup and restore

When you use Full Library Backup, VideoVault creates a backup locally on your device. You choose where to save it, such as iCloud Drive, On My iPhone/iPad, an external drive, or another file provider.

  • Backup files can contain videos, audio files, recordings, playlists, and media metadata.
  • VideoVault does not upload backup contents to a developer-operated server.
  • You are responsible for storing backup files in a trusted location and protecting access to them.

4. App deletion and device changes

Deleting VideoVault removes the app’s locally stored media and playlists from that device. Before deleting the app or changing devices, export a Full Library Backup to an external location. Off-device originals that remain in Photos, Files, or another storage service are not deleted by VideoVault.

5. Purchases

Lifetime Pro is a non-consumable in-app purchase processed by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. VideoVault receives transaction and entitlement status needed to unlock or restore Pro features. We do not receive your full payment card details.

6. Feedback and support email

VideoVault does not automatically collect your email address. If you choose to email support, your email address and the information you include are transmitted through your email provider and received at contact@videovault.de5.net. The address is routed to our support inbox through Cloudflare Email Routing.

Support messages are used to answer your request, investigate bugs, improve the app, and maintain reasonable support records. Do not include private media unless you intentionally choose to share it. You may request deletion of a support message by contacting us.

7. Analytics, advertising, and tracking

VideoVault 1.0 does not include third-party advertising, cross-app tracking, or a developer-operated analytics service. Apple may provide developers with aggregated App Store, purchase, and diagnostic information under Apple’s own terms and privacy practices.

8. Permissions

Depending on the feature you use, VideoVault may request access to Photos, Files, the microphone, and media playback capabilities. You can manage permissions in iOS or iPadOS Settings. Denying a permission may prevent the related feature from working but does not prevent unrelated features from operating.

9. Data retention and deletion

  • Local app data remains until you delete it in VideoVault, replace the library during restore, or delete the app.
  • Backup files remain in the location you selected until you delete them.
  • Support emails are kept only as reasonably needed for support, security, and legal recordkeeping.

10. Children

VideoVault is a general-purpose media utility and is not directed specifically to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through a developer-operated account or server.

11. Security

VideoVault uses Apple platform protections and app sandboxing. No storage or transmission method is completely secure, so protect your device, Apple Account, email account, and backup locations with appropriate security controls.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when VideoVault’s features or legal requirements change. The updated date will appear at the top of this page. Material changes will be reflected in the app or its App Store information when appropriate.

13. Contact

Privacy and support questions: contact@videovault.de5.net
