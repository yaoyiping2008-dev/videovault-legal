# VideoVault Support & Contact

Email: [contact@videovault.de5.net](mailto:contact@videovault.de5.net?subject=VideoVault%20Support)

We aim to respond to valid support requests within three business days.

## What we can help with

- Lifetime Pro purchase, entitlement, and Restore Purchase issues
- Media import, playback, Picture in Picture, and background playback
- Audio extraction, background music, volume adjustment, recording, export, and sharing
- Full Library Backup and Restore
- Bug reports, accessibility issues, and feature suggestions
- Privacy questions

## Information to include

- VideoVault version and build number
- Device model and iOS/iPadOS version
- A short description of what happened
- Steps that reliably reproduce the issue
- The result you expected

Do not send private media, passwords, Apple Account credentials, payment card information, or sensitive personal information.

## Backup and data recovery

Deleting VideoVault can remove locally stored media and playlists. Before deleting the app or changing devices, use **Settings → Data & Backup → Export Full Backup** and save the file to iCloud Drive or another external location.

**Restore Purchase** restores Lifetime Pro access. It does not restore media. Use **Restore from Backup** to restore media and playlists from a VideoVault backup file.

## Purchase and refund questions

Lifetime Pro is a non-consumable purchase processed by Apple. For billing or refund requests, use Apple’s purchase support channels. For an entitlement that should be active but is not, first use **Restore Purchase**, then contact us if the issue continues.

## Privacy

Support email is user-initiated. We use information you send only to respond, investigate the issue, improve VideoVault, and maintain reasonable support records. See the [Privacy Policy](https://privacy.videovault.de5.net/privacy.html).
