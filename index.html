📜 Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection Scope

VideoVault only collects minimal data required to operate media playback and playlist functions.

  • All media metadata is stored locally on your iPhone, no automatic cloud upload.
  • We will not read, copy or upload your local photos/videos without explicit permission.
  • Anonymous crash logs can be disabled in app settings, used only to optimize stability.

2. Data Storage & Security

Your media files are fully stored on your device. Uninstalling VideoVault will permanently erase all local app data, and we hold no copies of your personal media on remote servers.

3. User Rights

You have full rights to view, export or delete all data generated inside VideoVault at any time.
